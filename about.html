About Yash Laxmi Homes

Yash Laxmi Homes brings the philosophy of innovation, excellence and sustainability to the real estate industry. Each Yash Homes property combines a decade long legacy of excellence and trust with a commitment to cutting-edge design and technology.

Yash Laxmi Enterprises Pvt Ltd. is currently developing residential, commercial and township projects across Bihar with specific concentation in locations like Patna, Muzaffarpur and other emerging cities of Bihar by integrating cutting-edge design along with Tier-1 city amenities.

Over the past decade, Yash Laxmi Enterprises Pvt. Ltd has build multiple luxorious residential flats signifying unprecedented living combined with luxury and life style. Our commitment of building quality benchmark and Tier-1 city standard elite amenities provides our customers the life style and luxury hiterto unknown.
