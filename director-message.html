Director Message

Shri Dilip Thakur
CMD, Yash Laxmi Enterprises Pvt. Ltd.

Shri Dilip Thakur is the MD of Yash Laxmi Enterprises Private Limited, popularly known as Yash Laxmi Homes. Under his leadership, the Yash Laxmi Homes has been one of the most successful and respected conglomerates at Bihar in India for decades. Starting his career as an entrepreneur, he has marvelled his journey by turning all stones in his favor by his uncanny dedication and motivation directed towards excellence and transparency.

Mr. Dilip Thakur has been very much instrumental in driving the company growth from the very beginning and today his unprecedented growth and success story has become one the most admired story signifying entrepreneurship driven by absolute hard dedication and motivation.

Despite being the CMD of the company, he never thinks any work is worth not undertaking and is always gung-ho on new achievements. His Never Stop attitude keeps all of the working professionals motivated to Never Stop Working and gaining and achieving new heights of success everyday.

Building Yash Enterprises is just the beginning in the real estate market at Bihar in India. He intend to take Yash Laxmi Homes to international heights of success through his untiring attitude and commitment.
